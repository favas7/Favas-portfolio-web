RefleX

The world is moving fast and we are constantly left wanting for time and finding out things that can be postponed or cancelled just because we run out of time in the day. They say every second counts and we considered this saying in the best of letter and spirit. We normally spend about 10-20 minutes in-front of a mirror in our daily lives. We planned to make this time also productive and useful to the person using the mirror. We are moving towards a world with smart things and appliances. In the proposed idea we present an Interactive Smart Mirror named ”RefleX: IoT based Smart Solution”with various features to make sure that every minute of the user is utilized properly. The RefleX will be acting as a Personal Digital Assistant providing day-today schedule and appointments pulling the information from the user’s google account, it is also capable of displaying real time information such as date and time ,live weather updates,notifications with screen mirroring and also helps the person to get in touch with the current affairs happening around the world.A voice assistant can also be implemented with it. Further,RefleX can also be used to check the daily schedule using a to do list that will be displayed on the mirror.RefleX can be applied in various fields like Hotels,retail stores and also in workplace environment. It can be used in offices where it can be accessed by multiple users .RefleX is a Raspberry Pi based display which retrieves and displays the necessary information to the user using the internet.

Finca

Money management is an important part of our daily lives.Money management apps make it easier for customers to manage tasks such as tracking their expenses, bills and budgets. These apps are extremely easy to use as they are fully automated. Customers can rely on the technology built into their smartphones to seamlessly capture and organise their financial data. Money management apps are helping their customers to take important financial decisions and manage their money better on the go. This project is based on an expense and income tracking system. This project aims to create an easy, faster and smooth tracking system between the expense and the income. This project also offers some opportunities that will help the user to sustain all financial activities like digital automated diary. So, for the better expense tracking system, the developed project will help the users a lot. Most of the people cannot track their expense and income one way they face in money crisis, in this case daily expense tracker can help the people to tracking income-expense day to day and making life tension free. Money is the most valuable portion of our daily life and without money we will not last one day on this earth. So use of an daily expense tracker application is important to load happy family. Daily expense tracker helps the user to avoid unexpected expenses and bad financial situation. This Project will save time and provide a responsible lifestyle. This system is made using FLUTTER and FIREBASE and HIVE is used as database. Virtual Studio is used to develop the system. .
